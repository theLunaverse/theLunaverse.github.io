CAPTURING THE SKY

Launched on March 11, the SPHEREx space observatory has spent weeks undergoing checkouts and calibrations to ensure it is working as it should. Now it's mapping the entire sky to chart the positions of hundreds of millions of galaxies in 3D to answer some big questions about the universe.
